Built to dispatch the right engineer, fast.

Servora connects companies with verified IT engineers — network, cloud, security & system admin — with auto-assignment, live tracking, and OTP-verified completion. Four connected surfaces, fully admin-controlled.

End-to-end job flow

Customer books→ Auto-match (skill + location)→ Engineer accepts→ Navigate + Start OTP→ Work + Photo proof→ Completion OTP→ Escrow release + Rating

Tap any card to enter that surface. Inside each, components are clickable — tap a service, a job, a coupon, the online toggle — to see the next screen. Switch surfaces anytime from the top bar.

▸ Clickable: navbar links · service tiles → service detail · "Get started" / "Book a service" → opens Customer App · "Join as engineer" → Engineer App.
